ABOUT THIS


Synthwave Escape is a voluntary project, by a university student(me) for my grade(A? pls?).
It started out as a small project 12 weeks ago and has been growing ever since.

My mission is to provide an interactive, cute little website for visitors of all ages to experience
40 years of video game history. It uses an iconic neon "synthwave" style that looks both old
and futuristic, and draws inspiration from action, science-fiction, and horror films of the
1980s, as well as video games and art from the time period. Have fun.
